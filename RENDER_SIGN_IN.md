# How to Sign Into Render

## Step-by-Step Sign In

1. **Go to Render:**
   - Open: https://render.com
   - Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"** (top right)

2. **Choose Sign Up Method:**
   - **Option A: Sign up with GitHub** (Recommended)
     - Click **"Sign up with GitHub"**
     - Authorize Render to access your GitHub account
     - Render will have access to your repositories
   
   - **Option B: Sign up with Email**
     - Enter your email
     - Create a password
     - Verify your email
     - Then connect GitHub later

3. **After Signing Up:**
   - You'll be taken to the Render dashboard
   - You can see your services (empty at first)

## Why Sign Up with GitHub?

- **Easier deployment** - Render can directly access your repos
- **Automatic deployments** - Deploys on every push
- **No manual git setup** - Everything is connected

## After Signing In

Once you're signed in, you can:
1. Deploy your backend service
2. Deploy your frontend service
3. Set environment variables
4. View logs and monitor deployments
